Smart Buses

Our AI can track the location of every public bus in the city using computer vision, and provide the data to public to help them plan their journey better. The AI can also provide rash-driving analytics. The same technology can be used to make every bus-stand a smart bus-stand.

Public Transport Locator

Our AI can be used by the public to locate the wearabouts of all public transport (auto, buses, taxis, smart bikes, etc.) within a city, to plan their journey better.
