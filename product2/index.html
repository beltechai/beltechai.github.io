SecureMe Mobile App

If a person is walking alone at night and he/she feels unsafe, he/she can press a button on the app. He/She will be tracked.

His/Her live feed will be sent to her family/friends and also concerned authorities. If anyone suspicious is approching him/her or if there is threat or incident, we will notify the user by sending notification
