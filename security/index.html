Crime Detection

Our AI can automatically detect crime taking place in real time using computer vision. We can detect crime such as theft, harassment, physical flights, and a lot more, and alert the authorities immidiately.

Furthermore, our AI is always learning about new crimes, and thus improving over time.

Tracking of Suspected Criminals

Using the list of suspected criminals, we can track his moments, who he is meeting, where he is going, etc. We can also trigger if he changes his pattern, or does something suspicious.

Detailed analytics of every single suspected criminal is available at the fingertips of the relavant authorities.

Women Safety

Even before any kind of harrassment beings, our AI alerts the authorities about potential threats.

Our SecureMe app alerts people if there are any convicted criminals around their area. They can also use the app to share their wearabouts and movement with their friends and family.
