Smart Traffic Management

Our smart traffic management product, AI-ways, assesses in real-time the number of vehicles and pedestrians on each road and coordinates the traffic lights in a way such that the traffic moves the fastest. Smart traffic lights throughout the city also communicate with each other to ensure that the time spent on red lights is minimized.

Furthermore, our AI is always learning and improving to ensure the wait time at intersections reduce over time.

Traffic Violation Detection

Our state-of-the-art AI also detects traffic violations automatically using computer vision. We can detect speeding, traffic-light violation, one-way violation, no-helmet, illegal parking, and a lot more.

Our system is also integrated with the RTO's database and we can automatically generate challans.
