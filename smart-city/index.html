Garbage tracking

Our AI can automatically detect garbage across the city. It can track the person, place, and time of littering, and alert the authorities of such activities.

It can also track whether contractors are working properly and picking up garbage in their designated area.

Public infrastructure tracking

Our AI can detect any damage in public infrastructure (such as potholes, damaged pavements, etc.) and alert the respective authorities. It can also be used to monitor the efficiency of the relevant contractors at fixing the issues.

Smart Street Lights

Our AI can control the steet lights to ensure that they are only on when there is a person or a vehicle around. We can measure the speed and direction of movement, and coordinate intensity of street lights across the city to reduce electricity wastage.

The AI can also analyse the condition of every street light in the city and provide analytics.
